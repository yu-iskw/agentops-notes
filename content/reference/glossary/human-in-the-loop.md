---
title: Human-in-the-loop
---

Human-in-the-loop (HITL) is an approach where humans are integrated into AI systems to provide oversight, feedback, or intervention at critical decision points, ensuring human control and improving system performance through human expertise and judgment.

Sources:

- Holzinger: [Interactive Machine Learning for Health Informatics](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s40708-016-0042-6)
- Stanford HAI: [Human-Centered AI](https://hai.stanford.edu/research/human-centered-ai)
