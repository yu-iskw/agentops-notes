---
title: Hierarchical Planning
---

Hierarchical Planning is an approach to problem-solving where complex tasks are decomposed into multiple levels of abstraction, with high-level plans being refined into more detailed sub-plans, enabling efficient planning and execution of complex multi-step problems.

Sources:

- Nau et al.: [SHOP2: An HTN Planning System](https://www.jair.org/index.php/jair/article/view/10266)
- Russell and Norvig: [Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach](https://aima.cs.berkeley.edu/)
