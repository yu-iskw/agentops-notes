---
title: Content Filtering
---

Content Filtering in AI systems refers to the automated detection and removal or flagging of inappropriate, harmful, or unwanted content, including hate speech, misinformation, explicit material, or content that violates platform policies.

Sources:

- OpenAI: [Content Policy and Safety](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies)
- Perspective API: [Toxic Comment Classification](https://www.perspectiveapi.com/)
