---
title: Responsible AI
---

Responsible AI refers to the practice of developing, deploying, and governing artificial intelligence systems in a way that adheres to ethical principles, ensures fairness, transparency, accountability, and minimizes potential harm to individuals and society.

Sources:

- Partnership on AI: [Responsible AI Practices](https://partnershiponai.org/)
- Microsoft: [Responsible AI Principles](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/responsible-ai)
