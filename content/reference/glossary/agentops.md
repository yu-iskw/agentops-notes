---
title: AgentOps
---

AgentOps (Agent Operations) is the discipline focused on the deployment, orchestration, and monitoring of autonomous agents capable of real-time decision-making and multi-step task execution. It extends LLMOps by adding components for agent orchestration, safety monitoring, multi-agent coordination, and human-in-the-loop governance.
