---
title: Continual Learning
---

Continual Learning, also known as lifelong learning, is the ability of machine learning systems to continuously acquire new knowledge from a stream of data while preserving previously learned information, avoiding the problem of catastrophic forgetting.

Sources:

- Parisi et al.: [Continual Lifelong Learning with Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.07569)
- Aljundi et al.: [Memory Aware Synapses: Learning What (Not) to Forget](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.09601)
