---
title: Real-time Adaptation
---

Real-time Adaptation refers to the ability of AI systems to dynamically adjust their behavior, parameters, or decision-making processes in response to changing conditions or new information as it becomes available, without requiring offline retraining.

Sources:

- DeepMind: [Adaptive Agents and Multi-Agent Learning](https://deepmind.com/research/publications/multi-agent-learning)
- MIT Technology Review: [Real-Time AI Adaptation](https://www.technologyreview.com/2021/05/27/1025453/real-time-ai-adaptation/)
