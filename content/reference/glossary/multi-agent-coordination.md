---
title: Multi-Agent Coordination
---

Multi-Agent Coordination refers to the methods, algorithms, and communication protocols that enable multiple autonomous agents to collaborate, negotiate, and coordinate actions to achieve shared or complementary goals in dynamic environments.

Sources:

- Shoham and Leyton-Brown: [Multiagent Systems: Algorithmic, Game-Theoretic, and Logical Foundations](https://cs.stanford.edu/people/sohrab/mas/mas.pdf)
- SpringerLink: [Survey of Multi-Agent Coordination Techniques](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10458-020-09439-7)
