---
title: LLMOps
---

LLMOps (Large Language Model Operations) refers to the set of operational practices tailored to the deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of large-scale language models. It builds on MLOps by incorporating specialized components such as prompt engineering, resource optimization, fine-tuning techniques like LoRA, and robust ethical and compliance safeguards.
