---
title: Bias Detection
---

Bias Detection refers to the systematic identification and measurement of unfair or discriminatory patterns in machine learning models, data, or algorithms that may lead to unequal treatment of different groups or individuals.

Sources:

- Mehrabi et al.: [A Survey on Bias and Fairness in Machine Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.09635)
- IBM Research: [AI Fairness 360 Toolkit](https://aif360.mybluemix.net/)
