---
title: LLaMA
---

LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI) is a family of foundation language models developed by Meta, designed to be more efficient than other large language models while maintaining high performance across various natural language tasks.

Sources:

- Touvron et al.: [LLaMA: Open and Efficient Foundation Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.13971)
- Meta AI: [Introducing LLaMA](https://ai.meta.com/blog/large-language-model-llama-meta-ai/)
